║  📱 HOW TO USE                                                            ║
╠═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                           ║
║  For Employees (Job Seekers):                                            ║
║  1. Login to your account                                                ║
║  2. You'll land on Employee Dashboard                                    ║
║  3. Browse jobs using tabs (Part-Time, Full-Time, Daily)                 ║
║  4. Apply filters (location, salary, experience)                         ║
║  5. Click on job card to see details                                     ║
║  6. Click "Apply Now" to submit application                              ║
║  7. Track applications in "My Applications"                              ║
║                                                                           ║
║  For Employers:                                                          ║
║  1. Login to employer account                                            ║
║  2. Go to "Post Job" or "Create Job"                                     ║
║  3. Fill in job details (type, salary, location, etc.)                   ║
║  4. Publish - job appears instantly in employee dashboard                ║
║                                                                           ║
